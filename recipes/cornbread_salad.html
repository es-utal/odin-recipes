Cornbread Salad

Description

Cornbread Salad is a flavorful and layered dish made with crumbled cornbread, fresh vegetables like tomatoes, onions, and peppers, and a creamy dressing, often made with mayonnaise or ranch. It's typically served chilled and can be a perfect side dish for barbecues or potlucks. The combination of savory cornbread and fresh veggies creates a deliciously satisfying salad with a unique twist.
